Support and Resources

If you or someone you know is struggling with mental health, it is important to know that support is available. There are many organisations that provide advice, information, and emotional support for people experiencing stress, anxiety, or other mental health difficulties.

Finding Support for Mental Health

Accessing reliable information and support services can make a significant difference for individuals who are experiencing mental health issues. These organisations provide guidance, educational material, and confidential help for those who may need support

Helpful organisations

  • NHS Mental Health Information
  • Mind Mental Health Charity
  • Samaritans

These organisations provide advice, guidance, and support for people experiencing mental health difficulties.

Why Support Services Are Important

These services are designed to support people in different situations, whether they are looking for advice, emotional support, or professional guidance. Many organisations also provide resources for families and friends who want to support someone with mental health difficulties.
